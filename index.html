Welcome to the Beans vs Beans site! This is a weird FPS made by CliffracerX!

If you're interested in playing it, click the link for your platform (sorry mac users, haven't got a mac to test with so I'm putting mac support on hold) down below, following the pattern of OSname-32/64bit (x86=32 bit) Warning - this is likely rather buggy!

  • Windows x86
  • Windows x64
  • Linux x86
  • Linux x64

The default controls are: WASD/Arrow keys for movement Left mouse button/Left Control for firing your gun R for reloading your gun ESC for freeing your mouse cursor 1, 2, and 3 for taking out your 3 guns (look at the ammo counter in the top left)

To hop on a server if one is up, hit "refresh hosts" and click any new buttons that appear to join those servers.

Gameplay consists of a weird team deathmatch on a weird map, with some interior areas and outdoor platforms (on top of a layer of deadly goo), a red player's death is a blue point, and a blue player's death is a red point. Whichever team reaches the point goal first wins! There are ammo crates/medkits around the map to find and loot.
